最近忙着在搞“创作”， 很难想象像马斯克那些高经理精英人群每天可以处理那么多事情。
> Lately, I've been busy with "creating," and it's hard to imagine how top executives and elites like Elon Musk manage to handle so many tasks every day.

我自己一段时间貌似只能做一件事，想得慢做得也慢。
> I seem to be able to focus on only one thing at a time for a while, thinking and working slowly.

最近看到篇对电影《肖生克的救赎》的解读，其中一点就未雨绸缪，提前做局。电影和小说都是虚构，这种能力还是极其稀缺的。
> I recently came across an analysis of the movie "The Shawshank Redemption," which mentioned the idea of preparing in advance and setting things up ahead of time. Both the film and the novel are fictional, and this kind of ability is extremely rare.

我们日常的一些思考，其实尝试在用一种简单模型去理解和掌握事情，但是我们需要明白这种抽象方法在一定程度有好处，但是不能代表我们真的理解了一个复杂的问题，只是刚开始踏上了旅途。
> Some of our everyday thoughts actually attempt to use a simple model to understand and grasp things. However, we need to recognize that while this abstract approach has its advantages to some extent, it does not mean we truly understand a complex issue; it merely marks the beginning of our journey.
